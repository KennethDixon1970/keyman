Keyboards

Installation instructions

1

Press to select your keyboards

2

Open the 'Settings' app and go to 'General' > 'Keyboard'

3

Press 'Keyboards'

4

Press 'Add New Keyboard...'

5

Press 'FirstVoices' listed under 'Third Party Keyboards'

To use FirstVoices Keyboards

After completing these steps above, selected FirstVoices keyboards will be available on your iPhone or iPad by pressing the "Globe" button.
